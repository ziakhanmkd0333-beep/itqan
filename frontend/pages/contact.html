Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I apply for admission?

▼
You can apply through our online admission form. Simply navigate to the Admission page, select Student or Teacher application, fill out the required information in the multi-step form, and submit. Our team will review your application within 3-5 business days.

Are classes conducted online or in-person?

▼
All classes at Al-Itqan Institute are conducted online. We offer both one-to-one and group sessions via video conferencing platforms. This allows students from anywhere in the world to access quality Islamic education from qualified scholars.

Do you provide certificates upon course completion?

▼
Yes, we provide recognized certificates upon successful completion of courses. For specialized programs like Hifz-ul-Quran and advanced Hadith studies, certificates are issued with the approval of our qualified scholars including Dr. Noor Ur Rahman Hazarvi.

What are the class timings?

▼
We offer flexible timing options to accommodate students from different time zones. Classes are available in morning, afternoon, evening, and night slots. During the admission process, you can select your preferred time slot based on your availability.

Can I choose between male and female teachers?

▼
Yes, we have both male and female qualified teachers available. You can specify your preference during the admission process, and we will assign a suitable teacher based on your requirements and course selection.

What payment methods do you accept?

▼
We accept various payment methods including bank transfer, Easypaisa, JazzCash, and international wire transfers. Payment details will be provided once your admission is confirmed. Monthly fee ranges from $20-$70 depending on the course.

🌍 Global Online Institute

Al-Itqan Institute operates as a fully online Islamic educational platform, serving students worldwide. Our virtual classrooms connect you with qualified scholars regardless of your location.
