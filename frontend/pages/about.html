Introduction & Background

▼

His Eminence Sheikh Dr. Noor Ur Rahman Hazarvi is a distinguished researcher, hadith scholar, and expert in hadith sciences of the contemporary era, who has dedicated his scholarly life to the research and teaching of the Prophetic Hadith and its intricate sciences. He holds a prominent position in scholarly circles, particularly in the fields of Ilm al-Ilal (Science of Hidden Defects), Jarh wa Ta'dil (Criticism and Praise of Narrators), and Hadith Criticism due to his deep insight and research expertise.

Currently, he serves as the Head of the Specialization in Hadith Department at Jamia Al-Rashid Karachi and is also a member of the Specialization in Hadith Department at Jamia Haqqania Akora Khattak. He is also associated as the Coordinator of the MS (Islamic Studies – Hadith) program at Al-Ghazali University Karachi.

Education & Qualifications

▼

Dr. Noor Ur Rahman Hazarvi obtained his PhD and MPhil degrees in Hadith Sciences from the International Islamic University Islamabad with outstanding performance, achieving the highest grade (CGPA 4.0) and excellent percentages at both levels. He also earned an MA in Arabic from the same university and received a Gold Medal.

Earlier, he proved his exceptional scholarly abilities by securing first position in the entire Pakistan in the Shahadat al-Alimiyyah from Wafaq al-Madaris al-Arabiyyah Pakistan. He completed his Khārij (specialization) from Jamia Banori Town with the degree of Daktūra fī ʿUlūm as-Sunnah.

Teaching Experience

▼

His teaching experience is extensive and diverse. He has been associated with teaching Islamic sciences, history, and particularly Hadith sciences at various universities and educational institutions. He has served as a Visiting Lecturer at the University of Chakwal and the University of Peshawar, and as an Assistant Professor (Adjunct Faculty) at Al-Kawthar University Karachi.

Additionally, he served as a columnist for several years in the scholarly and intellectual magazine Wafaq al-Madaris, contributing insightful articles on various Islamic topics.

Areas of Expertise

▼

Dr. Hazarvi's primary field is Hadith and its critical sciences. He possesses particular expertise in teaching the following disciplines:

  • Ilm al-Tafsir (Science of Quranic Exegesis)
  • Ilm al-Ilal (Science of Hidden Defects)
  • Ilm al-Jarh wa Ta'dil (Science of Narrator Criticism)
  • Ulum al-Hadith
  • Usul al-Fiqh al-Hanafi (with practical applications)
  • Ilm al-Balagha (Rhetoric)
  • Arabic Literature
  • Nahw and Sarf (Grammar and Morphology)
  • Logic
  • Other Islamic and Arabic sciences

Research & Publications

▼

His contribution in the field of research and authorship is also significant. Several scholarly books and research studies have been published, particularly containing in-depth research on the critical methodologies of Imam Muslim, Imam Bukhari, Imam al-Daraqutni, and early hadith scholars.

His numerous research articles have been published in HEC-approved scholarly journals, discussing important topics such as Hadith Sciences, Mustalah al-Hadith, differences in chains and texts, and the relationship between Fiqh and Hadith.

Language Proficiency

▼

Linguistically, he has mastery over Arabic, Urdu, English, and Pashto languages. He has special expertise in Arabic oratory, scholarly writing, research guidance, and teaching Arabic to Arab students.

This multilingual proficiency enables him to communicate complex Islamic concepts effectively to students from diverse linguistic backgrounds and to access primary sources in their original languages.
